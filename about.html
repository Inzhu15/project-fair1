Why should you choose us?

"A person is what he eats" (Ludwig Feuerbach), therefore, the daily menu offered in the dining room is carefully thought out and differs in a variety of dishes and contains all the necessary set of products in terms of calories and vitamins: a free choice of dishes is offered daily, with a menu varied by day of the week, developed separately for breakfast, lunch and Dinner.There are several types of dishes that differ in the way of cooking: boiled, steamed, stewed, baked, fried, as well as a variety of salads. All dishes are checked for the quality of cooking and compliance with sanitary and hygienic requirements. The buffet offers a wide range of flour, confectionery and culinary products of its own production, hot and soft drinks, juices, compotes and much more, thus satisfying the taste of anyone who comes to have a snack or eat.
